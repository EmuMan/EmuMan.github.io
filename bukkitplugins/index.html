Bukkit Plugins

Minecraft is fun, but plugins can make it even more fun! Here are some of the plugins I've made.



InstaDeath

Remember what I said about making Minecraft more fun? Well now everything kills you in one hit! Your health will stay at 1/2 of a heart the whole time, and there's nothing you can do about it. The rest of the healthbar is even still there just to make you feel worse about your situation. Armor won't save you either, no matter how small the amount of damage you take is it will kill you. Instantly.
